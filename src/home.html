Features

Our website allows users to save streaming (anime, series, movies) and reading (books, manga, webtoons) content they want to see or read in a personal library. Users can also rate these contents based on how much they want to watch/read them using star ratings.

Interactive View

Explore a diverse range of entertainment options, from captivating anime series to gripping novels and webtoons. With HiStory's intuitive interface, you can effortlessly organize your favorites and stay updated on new releases.

Add and Consult

Add detailed information and dive into your favorite content with just a few clicks. HiStory provides dedicated pages for each entry, allowing you to access all the relevant information at a glance.
